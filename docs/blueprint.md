# **App Name**: St. Vincent Construction

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Visually appealing hero with a tagline and a call-to-action button to 'Request a Quote'. Includes an impactful background image of construction or a skyline.
- About Section: Elaboration on St. St. Vincent Construction Limited’s story and mission, expertise, commitment, and trustworthiness. Displays company address.
- Services & Projects Showcase: Details core services like general contracting, renovations, project management and showcase completed or ongoing projects using shadcn/ui Cards with image carousels inside Dialog components.
- Contact Form: A simple contact form (Name, Email, Message) with client-side validation and submit to email functionality.
- FAQ Section: An accordion-style FAQ section addressing common client questions using shadcn/ui's Accordion.
- Dynamic FAQ Tool: Use generative AI as a tool that is connected to the form on the contact page. Suggest an appropriate entry to the FAQ on the basis of form submissions from potential customers. Periodically retrain on these data in order to increase the relevance and improve results.
- Footer: A clean footer with copyright information, company address, and links to privacy/terms.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, trustworthy blue (#2C3E50) for a sense of reliability and professionalism.
- Background color: Light gray (#F9FAFB), near-white, creating a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Professional gold/cream (#E6B34B) used sparingly to highlight key elements and call-to-actions.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' for a modern, neutral, readable feel. Good choice for both headings and body.
- Use clean and professional icons related to construction, services, and contact information.
- Single-page layout with clear sections and smooth scrolling navigation, responsive breakpoints for optimal viewing on all devices.
- Subtle animations for hover states, transitions, and form submissions to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
